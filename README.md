☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard | Excel & Power BI Project
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance of a multi-location coffee shop business using interactive dashboards and business intelligence techniques. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights that help stakeholders understand revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and store-level efficiency.

🎯 Business Problem

Coffee shop management wanted to answer:

Which products generate the highest revenue?
What are the peak sales hours?
Which store performs best?
How are sales growing month-over-month?
What products should be promoted for higher profitability?

🔍 Key Insights
1. Strong Business Growth
Total Revenue: ₹6,98,812
Revenue increased by 103.8% from January to June 2023.
2. Coffee & Tea Drive Revenue
Coffee contributes 38.6%
Tea contributes 28.1%
Combined contribution: 66.7% of total revenue
3. Morning Hours Generate Maximum Sales
8 AM – 10 AM contributes 36.7% of daily revenue.
Peak-hour optimization can significantly impact overall sales.
4. Balanced Store Performance

Revenue is evenly distributed across all locations:

Hell's Kitchen
Astoria
Lower Manhattan

This indicates strong operational consistency across stores.

5. Premium Products Increase Profitability

Top premium products generated the highest revenue, showing customers are willing to pay more for quality offerings.

📈 Business Recommendations

✔ Increase staffing during morning peak hours

✔ Launch targeted promotions for top-performing coffee products

✔ Expand premium product offerings

✔ Replicate successful product strategies across all locations

✔ Use demand forecasting for inventory optimization
